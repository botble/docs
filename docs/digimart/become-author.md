# Becoming an Author

DigiMart allows any customer to become an author (seller) and publish products. The registration and approval process is straightforward.

## Eligibility

To become an author on your marketplace, you need:

- A customer account (email-verified)
- To be logged in
- To meet your marketplace's author policy (if any)

## Registration Steps

### 1. Access the Author Request Form

As a logged-in customer:

1. Navigate to your account area
2. Click **Become an Author** or **Seller Registration**
3. Review the author agreement and policies

### 2. Fill Author Application

Complete your seller profile with:

- **Display Name** — How you appear to customers (can differ from account name)
- **Bio** — Brief description of you/your company
- **Avatar** — Profile picture
- **Website** (optional) — Link to your personal or company website
- **Business Information** (optional) — Company name, registration number, tax ID

### 3. Agree to Terms

Confirm that you:
- Own the intellectual property of products you submit
- Comply with marketplace policies
- Accept marketplace terms of service

### 4. Submit Application

Click **Submit Application** to send your request to the admin.

You will receive a confirmation email that your application is being reviewed.

## Admin Review & Approval

After you submit your application, an admin reviews your profile:

- Admin checks for completeness and legitimacy
- Admin may approve, reject, or request changes
- You are notified via email of the decision

### If Approved

You gain the **Author** role and can immediately:
- Create and publish products
- Upload product versions
- Manage your author profile
- View download statistics and ratings

### If Rejected

Your application is declined, and you receive a reason via email. You can:
- Review the feedback
- Revise your profile
- Resubmit a new application

## Author Responsibilities

As an author, you agree to:

- **Quality Standards** — Submit functional, well-documented products
- **Copyright & Licensing** — Only publish content you own or have license to distribute
- **Responsive Support** — Respond to customer questions and feedback
- **Regular Updates** — Keep products updated and compatible
- **Policy Compliance** — Follow marketplace rules (no spam, malware, illegal content)

Violation of these responsibilities may result in:
- Product rejection
- Product deactivation
- Account warnings
- Author ban (temporary or permanent)

## Managing Your Author Profile

Once approved, manage your profile from your account:

1. Go to **My Account** → **Author Profile**
2. Edit:
   - Display name
   - Bio
   - Avatar
   - Website and business info
3. Click **Save**

Your profile is visible to customers on your product pages and author listing.

## Publishing Your First Product

After becoming an author, publish your first product:

1. Go to **My Products** → **Create Product**
2. Fill in product details (name, description, category, tags)
3. Choose a source (upload ZIP, GitHub, Envato)
4. Upload your product files/version
5. Submit for admin approval

See [Publishing Products](./publishing-products.md) for the complete workflow.

## Author Dashboard

Your author dashboard shows:

- **My Products** — List of your published, pending, and rejected products
- **My Earnings** — Revenue from paid products (if applicable)
- **Downloads** — Total and recent download counts
- **Ratings** — Your products' average ratings and reviews
- **Statistics** — Trends and performance metrics

Use this to track your marketplace presence and performance.

## Author Responsibilities & Support

### Respond to Customers

Engage with customer feedback:
- Reply to reviews and comments
- Answer questions about your products
- Address bugs or compatibility issues

### Keep Products Updated

Maintain your products:
- Release updates for bug fixes
- Ensure compatibility with new frameworks/versions
- Update documentation as needed

### Comply with Marketplace Rules

Follow all policies:
- Don't publish malware or harmful content
- Don't spam or mislead customers
- Don't violate copyrights or trademarks
- Don't engage in fraud or misrepresentation

## Account Bans

Authors who violate marketplace policies may be banned by the admin:

- **Temporary Ban** — Unable to publish for a set period; existing products are hidden
- **Permanent Ban** — Permanently removed from marketplace; all products hidden

A ban notification includes:
- Duration (days) or "permanent"
- Reason for the ban
- Contact info for appeals (if applicable)

## Account Suspension Recovery

If your account is suspended:

1. Review the ban reason carefully
2. Address the underlying issue (fix a product, remove content, etc.)
3. Contact admin support to request appeal
4. Admin may lift the ban if the issue is resolved

::: tip
Maintain a professional reputation. Respond promptly to customers, keep products updated, and follow marketplace rules. High-quality authors with good ratings are featured and promoted.
:::
