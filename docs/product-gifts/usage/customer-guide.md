# Customer Guide

This guide explains how customers experience free gifts when shopping on your store.

## How Free Gifts Work

When you shop at stores using Product Gifts, you can receive free products with your order when you meet certain purchase conditions.

## Seeing Gift Offers

### On Product Pages

Some stores show gift offers on individual product pages, letting you know what gifts you could receive.

### In Your Cart

The most common place to see gifts is in your shopping cart:

1. Add products to your cart
2. When your cart total reaches the minimum value, gift options appear
3. You'll see a "Free Gift" or "Your Gift" section

![Cart Gift Display](../images/cart-with-gifts.png)

### Gift Visibility

| Cart Status | Gift Display |
|-------------|--------------|
| Below minimum | Gifts not shown |
| Meets minimum | Gift options appear |
| Above minimum | Gift options remain visible |

## Selecting Your Gift

### Single Gift Option

If only one gift is available:
- It may be automatically selected
- Or displayed for confirmation
- Proceeds with your order

### Multiple Gift Options

If you can choose from multiple gifts:

1. You'll see all available gift products
2. Each shows product image and name
3. Original price shown struck through
4. "FREE" label indicates no cost
5. Select your preferred option

![Gift Selection](../images/product-gift-display.png)

### How to Choose

- Click on the gift option you want
- A checkmark or highlight indicates selection
- Your choice is remembered as you shop

## Gifts at Checkout

### Checkout Gift Selector

At checkout, you'll see your gift selection confirmed:

1. Gift section appears with your selection
2. You can change your choice if needed
3. Selected gift shows in order summary
4. Gift price shows as $0.00

### Confirming Your Gift

Before completing your order:
- Review your gift selection
- Change if desired
- Gift is locked in when you place order

## Multiple Gift Tiers

Some stores offer multiple gift levels:

**Example:**
- Spend $50 → Get a small gift
- Spend $100 → Get a medium gift (plus the small gift!)
- Spend $200 → Get a premium gift (plus all previous gifts!)

When you qualify for multiple tiers:
- You see gift options for each tier
- Select one gift from each tier
- All selected gifts are included in your order

## Gift in Your Order

### Order Confirmation

After checkout, your order confirmation shows:
- All products you purchased
- Gift products listed separately
- Gifts show $0.00 price
- Total doesn't include gift value

### Order History

In your account order history:
- Gifts appear as line items
- Marked as gift or promotional items
- $0.00 cost recorded

### Delivery

Your gift ships with your order:
- Included in the same package
- No additional shipping cost
- Treated like any other product

## Understanding Gift Conditions

### Minimum Order Value

Most gifts require a minimum purchase:
- Only products count (not shipping)
- Discounts may reduce qualifying amount
- Remove items = may lose gift eligibility

### Product-Specific Gifts

Some gifts only apply when buying certain products:
- Gift appears only with qualifying products
- Removing those products removes gift option
- Check promotion terms

### Category-Based Gifts

Some gifts apply to product categories:
- Buy from specific category to qualify
- Gift appears when category product is in cart
- Usually combined with minimum value

### Time-Limited Gifts

Some promotions have date restrictions:
- Available during promotional period only
- May end without notice
- Complete purchase to secure gift

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What if I change my cart?

If you remove items and drop below minimum:
- Gift options disappear
- Add more items to restore eligibility
- Previous selection remembered if you re-qualify

### Can I decline the gift?

In most cases:
- Gifts are automatically included
- Some stores allow opting out
- Contact store if you prefer no gift

### What if my gift is out of stock?

- Out-of-stock gifts are hidden automatically
- Choose from available options
- Gift product may be restocked later

### Can I exchange the gift?

- Gifts are promotional items
- Exchange policies vary by store
- Contact store customer service

### Do gifts have warranties?

- Standard product warranties typically apply
- Check store policy for specifics
- Treat as regular purchase for support

## Tips for Customers

### Maximizing Gift Benefits

1. **Check gift thresholds** before checkout
2. **Compare minimum values** to your cart total
3. **Add small items** if close to next tier
4. **Choose gifts you'll actually use**

### Before Completing Purchase

1. Verify your gift selection
2. Confirm cart meets minimum
3. Check that gift appears in order summary
4. Note gift is listed at $0.00

### After Receiving Order

1. Verify gift was included
2. Check gift product condition
3. Contact store if gift missing or damaged

## Need Help?

If you have questions about gifts:

1. **Check store's FAQ** for gift policies
2. **Contact customer service** for specific questions
3. **Review order details** for gift status

Common issues to report:
- Gift didn't appear in order
- Gift was damaged
- Wrong gift received
- Gift missing from package
