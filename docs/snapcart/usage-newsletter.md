# Newsletter

Collect customer email addresses and manage newsletter subscriptions.

## Setup

1. In admin panel, go to `Plugins` -> `Installed Plugins` and activate **Newsletter**
2. Newsletter subscription forms are built into the SnapCart theme

## Managing Subscribers

Go to `Newsletter` in the admin panel to view all subscribers. You can:

- View subscriber list with email and subscription date
- Export subscribers to CSV
- Delete subscribers

## Newsletter Form

SnapCart includes a newsletter subscription form partial that can be placed in:

- Footer area
- Homepage sections via shortcodes
- Any widget area

## Integration with Email Services

Export your subscriber list and import into your preferred email marketing platform (Mailchimp, SendGrid, etc.)
for campaign management.

::: tip
Offer an incentive (e.g., 10% off first order) to encourage newsletter sign-ups from mobile visitors.
:::
