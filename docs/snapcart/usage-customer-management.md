# Customer Management

Manage customer accounts, orders, and profiles from the admin panel.

## Viewing Customers

Go to `Ecommerce` -> `Customers` in the admin panel to view all registered customers.

## Customer Details

Click on a customer to view:

- **Profile**: Name, email, phone, address
- **Orders**: Complete order history
- **Wishlist**: Saved products
- **Reviews**: Submitted product reviews

## Creating a Customer

1. Go to `Ecommerce` -> `Customers`
2. Click `Create`
3. Fill in customer details (name, email, password)
4. Click `Save`

## Customer Account Features

Customers can manage their account from the frontend:

- View and edit profile information
- View order history and track orders
- Manage shipping addresses
- Access wishlist and compare lists
- Download invoices
- Change language and currency preferences

::: tip
SnapCart's mobile-optimized account pages make it easy for customers to manage their accounts on any device.
:::
