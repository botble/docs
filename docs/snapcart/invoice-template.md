# Invoice Template

Customize the appearance and content of order invoices.

## Configuration

Go to `Ecommerce` -> `Settings` -> `Invoice` in the admin panel.

## Customizable Fields

- **Company Name**: Your business name
- **Company Address**: Business address displayed on invoices
- **Company Phone**: Contact phone number
- **Company Email**: Contact email
- **Company Tax ID**: Tax identification number
- **Logo**: Upload your company logo
- **Footer Text**: Custom text at the bottom of invoices

## Invoice Layout

The invoice template includes:

- Company header with logo
- Invoice number and date
- Customer billing and shipping addresses
- Order items with quantities and prices
- Subtotal, tax, shipping, and total
- Payment method information
- Custom footer text

::: tip
Keep your invoice template professional and include all legally required information for your region.
:::
