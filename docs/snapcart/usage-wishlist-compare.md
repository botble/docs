# Wishlist & Compare

SnapCart allows customers to save products to a wishlist and compare products side by side.

## Wishlist

Customers can add products to their wishlist by clicking the heart icon on product cards or product detail pages.

### Viewing Wishlist

- Customers access their wishlist from the account dashboard or bottom navigation
- Wishlist items show product image, name, price, and stock status
- Items can be added to cart or removed directly from the wishlist

## Compare

Product comparison allows customers to compare features and specifications of multiple products.

### Using Compare

1. Click the compare icon on a product card
2. Add up to 4 products for comparison
3. View the comparison table showing attributes side by side

## Managing as Admin

Wishlist and compare data is tracked per customer account. No admin configuration is needed beyond ensuring the
ecommerce plugin is active.

::: tip
Wishlist is especially valuable on mobile where customers browse frequently but may purchase later. It helps
reduce cart abandonment.
:::
