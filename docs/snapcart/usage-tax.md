# Tax

Configure tax rates for your products based on location and product type.

## Managing Tax Rules

Go to `Ecommerce` -> `Tax` in the admin panel.

## Creating a Tax Rule

1. Click `Add new tax`
2. Fill in the details:

- **Name**: Tax name (e.g., VAT, GST)
- **Percentage**: Tax rate percentage
- **Priority**: Priority order when multiple taxes apply
- **Country/State**: Apply tax to specific locations

3. Click `Save`

## Tax Display

SnapCart can display prices with or without tax. Configure this in `Ecommerce` -> `Settings` -> `General` -> **Tax display**.

::: tip
Consult your local tax authority for the correct tax rates applicable to your products and region.
:::
