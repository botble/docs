# Floating Contact

SnapCart includes floating contact buttons that allow customers to quickly reach you via popular messaging channels.
The buttons appear as a fixed overlay on all pages.

## Supported Channels

- **Zalo**: Opens Zalo chat with your store
- **Phone**: Initiates a phone call
- **Messenger**: Opens Facebook Messenger chat

## Configuration

To configure floating contact buttons, go to `Appearance` -> `Theme Options` -> `Contact Information` in the admin panel.

Fill in the following fields:

- **Phone Number**: Your store's phone number
- **Zalo**: Your Zalo ID or link
- **Facebook**: Your Facebook page URL (used for Messenger)

## Enable/Disable

You can toggle the floating contact feature on/off in `Appearance` -> `Theme Options` -> `Features` -> **Floating Contact**.

::: tip
Floating contact buttons are especially effective on mobile where customers prefer messaging over email. Keep your
response times fast to maximize conversions.
:::
