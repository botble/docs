# Paying with Your Wallet at Checkout

This guide shows you how to use your wallet balance to pay for orders.

## Before You Start

To pay with your wallet, you need:

1. **An account** - You must be logged in
2. **Wallet balance** - You need enough money in your wallet
3. **Items in cart** - Something to buy!

## How to Pay with Wallet

### Step 1: Add Items to Cart

Shop as usual and add items to your cart.

### Step 2: Go to Checkout

Click **Checkout** when you're ready to pay.

### Step 3: Choose Wallet Payment

On the payment method section, you'll see:
- Your available wallet balance
- Option to pay with **Wallet** or **E-Wallet**

Click to select wallet as your payment method.

### Step 4: Review Your Order

Check:
- Items in your order
- Total amount
- Your wallet balance will cover it

### Step 5: Confirm Payment

Click **Place Order** or **Confirm**

**What happens:**
- Money is deducted from your wallet instantly
- Your order is confirmed
- You see the success page

### Step 6: Done!

Your order is complete! You'll receive:
- Order confirmation
- Updated wallet balance
- Transaction in your wallet history

## What You'll See at Checkout

When wallet payment is available, you'll see:

```
Payment Method:
○ Credit Card
○ PayPal
● Wallet (Balance: $150.00)
```

Your wallet balance is shown so you know how much you have.

## When You DON'T Have Enough Balance

If your order costs more than your wallet balance:

**What you'll see:**
- Wallet option may be grayed out
- Message: "Insufficient balance"
- Your current balance vs order total

**What to do:**

1. **Add money first** - Go to your wallet and top-up
2. **Use another method** - Pay with credit card or PayPal instead

::: tip
You cannot split payment between wallet and another method. The wallet must cover the full amount.
:::

## After Your Purchase

### Your Wallet Shows

- **Transaction type:** Payment
- **Amount:** The order total (negative, since money left)
- **Balance after:** Your new balance

### Your Order Shows

- **Payment method:** Wallet
- **Payment status:** Paid

## Getting Refunds

If you return an item or cancel an order:

1. Refund goes directly to your wallet
2. You'll see a "Refund" transaction
3. Money is available immediately
4. Use it for future purchases or withdraw

::: info Refunds Are Automatic
When the store processes your refund, it goes to your wallet automatically. You don't need to do anything!
:::

## Tips for Checkout

### Check Your Balance First

Before checkout, make sure you have enough in your wallet. You can:
- View balance on your wallet page
- The balance also shows at checkout

### Save Time with Wallet

Using wallet means:
- No entering card numbers
- No 3D Secure verification
- Instant payment confirmation

### Keep Some Balance

Consider keeping a balance in your wallet if you shop frequently. This makes checkout faster every time!

## Common Questions

### Why can't I see the wallet option?

Possible reasons:
- You're not logged in (wallet requires login)
- Wallet feature is currently disabled
- Your balance is $0

### Why can't I select wallet?

Possible reasons:
- Your balance is lower than the order total
- Wallet payment might be temporarily unavailable

### What if payment fails?

Wallet payments rarely fail since it's just moving your stored balance. If it does:
- Your wallet balance is NOT deducted
- You can try again
- Contact support if it keeps happening

### Can I pay part with wallet and part with card?

Currently, no. You must pay the full amount with one method. Either:
- Use 100% wallet, OR
- Use 100% another payment method

### Is wallet payment refundable?

Yes! If you cancel or return your order, the refund goes back to your wallet automatically.
