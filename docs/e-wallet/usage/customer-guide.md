# Customer Guide: Using Your Wallet

This guide is for customers who want to use the wallet feature on your store.

## What is Your Wallet?

Your wallet is like a digital account where you can store money to shop faster. Think of it as a prepaid balance for the store.

**Benefits:**
- Pay instantly without entering card details every time
- Get refunds faster (straight to your wallet)
- Keep track of all your transactions in one place

## Accessing Your Wallet

1. Log in to your account on the store
2. Go to **My Account**
3. Click on **My Wallet** or **E-Wallet**

You'll see:
- Your current balance
- Your transaction history
- Buttons to add money or withdraw

## Adding Money to Your Wallet (Top-up)

Want to add money to your wallet? Here's how:

### Step 1: Start the Top-up

1. Go to your wallet page
2. Click **Top Up Wallet** or **Add Money**

### Step 2: Enter the Amount

1. Type the amount you want to add
2. Or click a quick amount button (like $50, $100)

::: tip Limits
There may be minimum and maximum amounts you can add at once. The page will show you these limits.
:::

### Step 3: Choose Payment Method

Select how you want to pay:
- Credit/Debit Card
- PayPal
- Bank Transfer
- Other options available on your store

### Step 4: Complete Payment

1. Follow the payment instructions
2. Complete the payment with your chosen method
3. Once payment is confirmed, money appears in your wallet immediately!

### Step 5: Confirmation

You'll see:
- Updated wallet balance
- A confirmation message
- The transaction in your history

## Checking Your Balance

Your balance is always shown at the top of your wallet page.

**What your balance means:**
- This is money you can spend at the store
- It's stored safely in your account
- You can use it anytime you shop

## Viewing Your Transactions

Your wallet page shows all your transactions:

| Type | What It Means |
|------|---------------|
| **Top-up** | You added money |
| **Payment** | You paid for an order |
| **Refund** | You got money back from a return |
| **Withdrawal** | You moved money out of your wallet |

Each transaction shows:
- The date and time
- The amount
- Your balance before and after

## Withdrawing Money

Want to get money out of your wallet? You can request a withdrawal.

### Step 1: Start the Withdrawal

1. Go to your wallet page
2. Click **Withdraw** or **Request Withdrawal**

### Step 2: Enter Details

1. Enter the amount you want to withdraw
2. Choose your payout method:
   - **Bank Transfer** - Money goes to your bank account
   - **PayPal** - Money goes to your PayPal account
3. Enter your payment details (bank account, PayPal email, etc.)

### Step 3: Submit Request

1. Review your request
2. Click **Submit**
3. You'll see a confirmation

### Step 4: Wait for Processing

- The store will review your request
- This usually takes 1-3 business days
- You'll be notified when it's approved
- Money is sent to your account after approval

### What If My Withdrawal Is Rejected?

Don't worry! If rejected:
- The money goes back to your wallet immediately
- You can try again with correct details
- Contact support if you need help

## Tips for Using Your Wallet

### Keep Your Account Secure

- Use a strong password
- Don't share your login with others
- Log out on shared computers

### Check Your Balance

- Review your transactions regularly
- Make sure all transactions are ones you made
- Contact support if you see anything strange

### Manage Your Balance

- Only add what you plan to spend soon
- You can always add more later
- Remember you can withdraw if needed

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is my money safe?

Yes! Your wallet balance is stored securely and protected by your account password.

### Can my wallet balance expire?

Check with the store - policies may vary. Usually, wallet balances don't expire.

### What if I forget my password?

Use the password reset feature to regain access. Your wallet balance will still be there!

### Can I transfer money to another customer?

This feature is currently not available. Each wallet is personal to your account.

### What currency is my wallet in?

Your wallet uses the store's main currency. You'll see the currency symbol on your wallet page.

### Is there a fee to use the wallet?

Using the wallet is usually free. However, some payment methods for adding money may have fees from their providers.

## Need Help?

If you have questions or problems:

1. Check the store's FAQ section
2. Contact customer support
3. Use the live chat if available

**Common issues support can help with:**
- Payment didn't go through
- Balance seems incorrect
- Withdrawal questions
- Account access problems
