# How to Use E-Wallet

Welcome to the E-Wallet user guide! This section will help you understand how to use the wallet system in your online store.

## What is E-Wallet?

E-Wallet is like a digital piggy bank for your customers. They can:

- **Add money** to their wallet (top-up)
- **Pay for orders** using their wallet balance
- **Receive refunds** directly to their wallet
- **Withdraw money** back to their bank account

## Quick Start

### For Admins

1. Go to your admin panel
2. Click **E-Wallet** in the menu
3. You'll see the dashboard with all wallet statistics

### For Customers
