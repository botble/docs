# Velura - Spa, Beauty & Nail Salon Booking Theme

## Introduction

Velura is a premium, multi-page theme crafted exclusively for the beauty and wellness industry. Built on Botble CMS, it provides a complete solution for spas, beauty salons, nail bars, massage studios, wellness centers, and independent beauty professionals looking to establish an elegant digital presence with online appointment booking.

Whether you run a luxury day spa, a boutique nail studio, or a full-service beauty salon, Velura blends refined aesthetics with powerful functionality. Its calm, modern, and elegant design ensures a seamless experience that turns visitors into booked clients.

Built on the powerful Bootstrap Grid System and leveraging the flexibility of Botble CMS, Velura delivers fully responsive layouts that look flawless across all devices - from desktops to tablets and mobile phones.
