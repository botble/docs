# Theme Options

Theme options are a great way to customize your theme. You can change the colors, typography, layout, and more.

To access the theme options, go to `Appearance` -> `Theme Options` in your admin panel.

![Theme Options](./images/theme-options.png)

## General

The **General** tab allows you to configure fundamental settings that shape your website's identity and basic operation,
such as the site name, description, and other essential details.

![General](./images/theme-options.png)

## Logo

The **Logo** tab allows you to set the logo, dark logo, favicon, and logo height.

![Logo](./images/theme-options/logo.png)

## Header

The **Header** tab lets you adjust header visibility, text and background colors, choose between header styles, enable a
transparent header, and configure action buttons with custom labels, URLs, and icons.

![Header](./images/theme-options/header.png)

### Header Transparent

When this feature is enabled, the header has a transparent background and floats over the top of the page (great for the homepage hero).

Demo when `enabled`:

![Header Transparent On](./images/header-transparent-on.png)

Demo when `disabled` (inner pages use a solid header):

![Header Transparent Off](./images/header-transparent-off.png)

## Styles

The **Styles** tab lets you adjust the primary color, secondary color, heading color, and text color.

![Styles](./images/theme-options/styles.png)

## Footer

The **Footer** tab lets you adjust the background color, bottom background color, border color, text colors, and background image.

![Footer](./images/theme-options/footer.png)

## Breadcrumb

The **Breadcrumb** tab lets you adjust breadcrumb visibility, text and background colors, and choose between breadcrumb styles.

![Breadcrumb](./images/theme-options/breadcrumb.png)

## Page

You can specify the pages to use as the homepage and galleries page here.

![Page](./images/theme-options/page.png)

## Typography

The **Typography** tab lets you adjust the font family.

![Typography](./images/theme-options/typography.png)

## Blog

The **Blog** tab lets you adjust the number of posts, categories, and tags per page, the display style, post item style, and more.

![Blog](./images/theme-options/blog.png)

## Tour Manager

The **Tour Manager** tab lets you configure tour-specific display options — tours per page, listing layouts, booking
behavior, and related settings for the tour booking experience.

![Tour Manager](./images/theme-options/tour-manager.png)

## Map Settings

The **Map Settings** tab lets you configure the map provider and API key used to display destination and tour locations.

![Map Settings](./images/theme-options/map.png)

## Social Links

Social links are a great way to connect with your audience. Add your social media links in the **Social Links** tab.

![Social Links](./images/theme-options/social-links.png)

## Social Sharing

Enable social sharing buttons so visitors can share your tours and posts. Configure them in the **Social Sharing** tab.

![Social Sharing](./images/theme-options/social-sharing.png)

## Newsletter Popup

The **Newsletter Popup** tab lets you enable and configure a newsletter subscription popup.

![Newsletter Popup](./images/theme-options/newsletter-popup.png)

## Toast Notification

The **Toast Notification** tab lets you enable and configure toast notifications shown on the frontend.

![Toast Notification](./images/theme-options/toast-notification.png)

## Cookie Consent

The **Cookie Consent** tab lets you enable and customize the cookie consent banner.

![Cookie Consent](./images/theme-options/cookie-consent.png)
