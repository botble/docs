# Customer Guide - Shopping Wholesale

This guide is for customers who want to purchase products at wholesale prices. Learn how to apply for a wholesale account and take advantage of special pricing.

## What is Wholesale?

Wholesale is a special pricing program for business customers who buy in larger quantities. Instead of paying retail prices, you get discounted rates based on:

- Your customer group membership
- The quantity you purchase
- Your order volume commitments

**Benefits:**
- Lower prices than retail
- Volume discounts - buy more, save more
- Access to wholesale-only products
- Priority customer support (if offered)

## Who Can Apply?

Wholesale accounts are typically available to:

- **Retailers** - Stores reselling products
- **Distributors** - Companies distributing to retailers
- **Resellers** - Online sellers and marketplaces
- **Corporate Buyers** - Businesses buying for their operations
- **Non-profits** - Organizations with tax-exempt status

Requirements vary by store, but typically include:
- Valid business registration
- Tax ID or business number
- Physical business address
- Minimum purchase commitments

### Step 2: Fill Out Application Form

**Business Information:**

## Understanding Pricing

### Your Base Discount

As a wholesale customer, you're assigned to a customer group that gives you a base discount on all products.

**Example Groups:**
- Bronze: 10% off
- Silver: 15% off
- Gold: 20% off
- Platinum: 25% off

### Volume Pricing

Many products offer additional discounts for larger quantities:

**Example Product - Widget ($100 retail):**

| Your Group | 1-49 units | 50-99 units | 100+ units |
|------------|------------|-------------|------------|
| Silver (15% off) | $85 | $80 | $75 |
| Gold (20% off) | $80 | $75 | $70 |

**The More You Buy, The More You Save!**

## Minimum Order Quantities (MOQ)

Some products have minimum order quantities:

**Example:**
- Product: Premium Widget
- MOQ: 24 units
- Must buy in multiples of: 12

**Valid Orders:** 24, 36, 48, 60...
**Invalid Orders:** 10, 25, 30, 50...

::: tip Automatic Adjustment
If you try to order an invalid quantity, the system will automatically adjust to the next valid amount.
:::

## Common Questions

### Can I buy just one unit?

Some products have minimum order quantities. Check each product's MOQ requirement. Many products allow single-unit purchases for wholesale customers.

### Why don't I see the pricing table?

Pricing tables only appear on products that have volume discounts. If you only see your group discount, that product doesn't have additional tiered pricing.

### Can I place orders for multiple locations?

Yes! You can add multiple shipping addresses in your account and ship different items to different locations in a single order.

### What if I need to return something?

Return policies vary by store. Check the store's return policy or contact customer service. Wholesale orders may have different return terms than retail.

### How often can I reorder?

As often as you like! There's typically no limit on order frequency, only minimum order amounts per order.

### Can I get better pricing?

Contact your account manager to discuss:
- Volume commitments
- Annual contracts
- Exclusive products
- Custom pricing tiers
